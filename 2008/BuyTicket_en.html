Purchasing Tickets to RubyKaigi 2008

Tickets to RubyKaigi 2008 go on sale through Lawson Ticket from 10am on Saturday, May 5. RubyKaigi 2008 tickets are sold out. Thank you!

(Overseas attendees: Please contact the Ruby Kaigi 2008 Executive Committee via e-mail for assistance. Applications via e-mail will close on May 25th at 2p.m JST.)

Regarding RubyKaigi 2008 Ticketing and Schedule

RubyKaigi 2008 will be held over 3 days, from Friday, June 20 until Sunday, June 22. The conference will take place in two different halls within the Tsukuba International Conference Center, each with a different schedule.

  • Friday, June 20 - Multi-Purpose Hall only
  • Saturday, June 21 - Both Main Hall and Multi-Purpose Hall
  • Sunday, June 22 - Both Main Hall and Multi-Purpose Hall

Tickets are ONLY required for the Main Hall. Specifically:

  • Saturday, June 21 - Main Hall (Main Sessions)
  • Sunday, June 22 - Main Hall (Main Sessions)

In summary:

  • Tickets are required on Day 1 (Saturday, June 21) and Day 2 (Sunday, June 22) for the main sessions in the Main Hall
  • Tickets are not required on Day 0 (Friday, June 20), Day 1 (Saturday, June 21) and Day 2 (Sunday, June 22) for events in the Multi Purpose Hall.

Ticket holders may present their tickets in exchange for RubyKaigi 2008 novelty items at the Main Hall reception from Day 1 (Saturday, June 21) onwards.

Tickets are limited, so please purchase your tickets early! Tickets are sold out. Thank you!

Dinner Party Tickets

The RubyKaigi 2008 official dinner party to be held on the night of Day 1 will require the purchase of a separate ticket.
