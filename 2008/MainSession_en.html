Program - Main Sessions

The main sessions are presentations to be held in the Main Convention Hall of the Tsukuba International Congress Center on Saturday, June 21 and Sunday, June 22. (Session times, speakers and session titles are subject to change.)

You will need to purchase a ticket in advance to attend the main sessions.

The sub-sessions will be run in parallel in the Multi-Purpose Hall.
