The Official Ruby Kaigi 2008 Dinner Party

The official Ruby Kaigi 2008 dinner party will be held from 7 p.m. on the night of Day 1, Saturday, June 21.

You must purchase a ticket in advance to attend. As with tickets to the main conference, tickets to the dinner party will go on sale via Lawson Ticket at 10a.m. on Saturday, May 5. Please note that this ticket is separate from the main conference ticket, and has a different L-code.

The party will be held in a room at the same venue as RubyKaigi 2008, inside the Tsukuba International Convention Center.

Tickets are limited, so please purchase your tickets early. Tickets are sold out. Thank you!

Ticket Details

Please note that the dinner party tickets do not share an L-code with the conference tickets.
